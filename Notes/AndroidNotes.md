## Screen densities and Splash screen sizes

On these screen densities, the splash screen is at least this high and wide:

    ldpi - 240x360px
    mdpi - 320x480px
    hdpi - 480x720px
    xhdpi - 640x960px
    xxhdpi - 960x1440px
    xxxhdpi - 1280x1920px
